Developer Resources

There is a huge amount of material out there, a curated collection of tools, emulators, and communities for Amiga developers would be an impossible endeavor. Not to mention that each engineer has their own unique workflow and preferences. So this list is around what I usually end up using.

Compilers & Tools

  • amiga-gcc: GCC cross-compiler for m68k Amiga development.
  • VBCC: Optimizing C compiler for Amiga systems.
  • vAmiga: User-friendly Amiga 500/1000/2000 emulator for macOS. (Website)
  • FS-UAE: Cross-platform Amiga emulator with a focus on accuracy.

Documentation & Tutorials

  • Amiga Developer Docs: Official AmigaOS programming documentation.
  • Amiga Coding: Tutorials for 68k assembly and C programming.

Communities

  • English Amiga Board: Active forum for developers and enthusiasts.
  • Amiga.org: Community for Amiga support and discussions.

Self Enjoyment

  • A great soundtracker: For background white noise and ocassional f-yeah moments!.
  • Amiga Scenes: A curated collection of Amiga scenes.
