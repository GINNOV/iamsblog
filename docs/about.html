About the Amiga Dev Hub

My fascination with the Amiga began in the 1980s, drawn to its groundbreaking hardware and vibrant developer community. From coding demos in assembly to exploring AmigaOS, the Amiga shaped my love for programming.

This site is a resource for developers who want to code for the Amiga, whether on original hardware or emulators like vAmiga. It’s a place to find tools, share knowledge, and celebrate the Amiga’s legacy.

Have ideas or resources to share? Connect with me on the English Amiga Board.
